02 · THE STUDIO

Small. Sharp. Serious.

One founder. A stack of tools nobody else is building. Every product shipped here survived being used daily by its author before anyone else saw it.

We don't pitch decks. We don't raise rounds. We don't "go to market." We build things we'd pay for ourselves, then we let strangers pay for them too.

Biskin Studio is a solo operation out of California running on AI agents, trading systems, motion design, and the stubborn belief that a single person with the right tools can out-ship a 30-person company that is "aligning around OKRs."

If our work looks expensive, it's because every pixel costs something — time, taste, or both. If it looks loud, it's because we'd rather be remembered than agreeable.
