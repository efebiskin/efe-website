Three steps, one week each. Fixed fee, fixed scope, async-first. You see motion proof of life on Day 3, a shippable draft by Day 10, a launched site by Day 14.

01 — Week 01

60-minute intake. You share positioning, audience, and any existing brand. You get back two directional moodboards with typography, palette, and motion vocabulary within 48 hours.
