// Specifications

The numbers, without theatre.

9,800 TOPS · INT8

Sustained, not peak. Measured under continuous load with the standard MLPerf inference suite at 25°C.

12W TDP · TYPICAL

Passive cooling at 50°C ambient. No fan, no liquid loop, no thermal throttling under stated workload.

384 CORES · TILED

Eight clusters of 48 SIMD-32 cores. Mesh interconnect. 1.4 TB/s aggregate bisection bandwidth.

128GB UNIFIED MEMORY

HBM3e on-package, 4.8 TB/s. Single coherent address space across host + accelerator.

7nm PROCESS · TSMC N7

Mature node by intent. Production yield, conservative defect density, lower BOM.

A0 SILICON · TAPED OUT

First silicon back from the fab Feb 2026. Bring-up complete. Two known errata, both software-mitigated.
